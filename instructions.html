Kiosk Journey

The theme is Space Mission, and each kiosk is associated with a planet from the game.

There are two categories of kiosk
  1. Presentation
  2. Video (DIY). Name of a particular kiosk and it's category is associated with a character.

Do not forget the planets,In case you forget, please verify it with the kiosk image or click on to view the descriptions of all the kiosks Once you click on to know more about a particular kiosk,please click on More button which is placed below the character. To attend a particular kiosk,please remember the character, and click on the same character in the homepage. Once you click on the character, you can view the respective kiosk details along with 3 quiz questions.

Answering quiz questions is not mandatory however if you get your answers right, you will earn extra points. Post which you are required to rate the kiosk, which is mandatory and then you will be given a batch PIN by the presenter. you need to type the session code to mark the particular kiosk as completed.The above process repeats for all kiosks.
